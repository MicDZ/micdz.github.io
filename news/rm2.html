RoboMaster University Championship Regionals

May 7, 2024

The RoboMaster University Championship (RMUC) gives participants the opportunity to comprehensively improve their practical engineering skills, integrating various robotics-related disciplines such as machine vision, embedded system design, mechanical engineering, autonomous navigation, and human-machine interaction. The fashionable presentation format makes RMUC even more exciting, drawing in viewers from all walks of life and spreading the love of robotics even further.

In the RMUC, participating teams will independently develop different types of robots with various functions to face off on the battlefield. Each team’s participants will control their robots to attack the opponent’s robots and Base by launching projectiles. At the end of the match, the team with the highest remaining Base HP wins.
